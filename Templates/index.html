We Are Team Juggernauts

Discipline, determination and dedication

2017 marked the first time Team Juggernauts participated in a national level Go-Kart event. Comprised of 23 highly dedicated members from Sant Longowal Institute of Engineering and Technology, Longowal(Punjab), the key objective of this team was to learn, design and fabricate a Go-Kart racing vehicle that was safe, comfortable, inexpensive and asthetically appealing. Under the Guidance of our faculty advisor Dr. Shankar Singh and the support of SLIET, the team has won various awards in the coming years ever since.

Departments

Departments

Design

A designer handles the overall design of the chassis of the vehicle. He also performs various types of analysis on different parts of the vehicle to test their viability.

Power Transmission

The engine, gears, clutch and other parts involved in the transmission of power from the engine to the rear wheels of the vehicle are handled by this dept. This department requires deep knowledge of the engine, gears, axle and the related calculations.

Steering

The department handles the steering of the vehicle. Different types of steering mechanisms and a good knowledge of the center of mass of bodies is essential. Steering also involves long calculations, and one is expected to handle them

Brake

The braking system of the vehicle is handled by this dept. Deep knowledge of the brake disc and how it deforms under heat and pressure, different types of brake fluids, mechanism of different types of brakes, especially the hydraulic brake is required in this field.
